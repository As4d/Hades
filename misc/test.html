About Hades

This is a testing trojan virus where users can launch on their machine to see if they’re properly protected if they were to be infected. It has the same capabilities of a real trojan virus but does not damage or steal actual information. The app gives users tips on ways to protect themselves after the simulation happens for example renaming files, deleting files and recommending to block certain devices that connect to a port. It gathers data and visualizes it in a clean and simple manner for the user to preview and gives the user a security score between 1 to 5, 1 being extremely at risk with no security and 5 being extremely secure.
